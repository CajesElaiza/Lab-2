"Making Pancakes"

Needed ingredients:

  • 1 cup of flour
  • 1/2 cup of milk
  • an egg
  • 1 cup of water
  • 1/4 cup of butter
  • 2 tablespoon of sugar

Procedures:

  1. Mix all the ingredient in a huge bowl until big lumps of the mixture has been dissolved.
  2. Heat the pan in medium high heat. Brush the pan with just a little amount of butter.
  3. Pour some amount of the mixture into the pan. Wait until bubbles appear and side looks dry.
  4. Repeat the procedure (No.3) until all of the mixture has been cooked.
  5. When pancakes are on the plate, put some butter and syrup. You may now enjoy your pancakes!

Note: If ever you desire some flavored pancakes (such as chocolate, strawbery, blueberry and banana), you may add some bars or bits of fruits in the mixture. Work on your cooking talent! ♥ ♥ ♥
